Use your Beeline mobile balance to pay for products and services at Simply.

Just link your Beeline mobile balance to Simply. Top it up without a fee:

  • In «My Beeline» app, «Top up» tab
  • Via any Internet banking, terminals and cash desks

Then you can make purchases and transfer from your balance. Nothing extra has to be done :)

You can also top up Simply with your bank card. You can do this both in our app and in bank’s apps. However, your bank may withhold an additional fee.
